Bella Boots are made of a durable fabric, Cordura, which was first used in World War II to manufacture tires. The weight and quality of the Cordura used in Bella Boots is carefully selected to balance between a fabric that wears well on city streets, yet is supple enough to feel natural on the dog's paw. Cordura has a waterproof coating.

The unique design of Bella Boots has a higher back, so as the dog walks and steps on the fabric, there is extra height to prevent the back of the boot slipping below the strap and falling off. With Bella Boots you can enjoy being outside in winter without the frustration and hassle of losing boots.

Bella Boots are color-coded by size (an idea "borrowed" from how hospital scrubs are organized). If you have more than one dog, it is easy to keep track of whose boot is whose! Also, you don't have to remember the size your dog wears, just tell us what color trim.

Bella Boots uses a stretchy Velstretch strap and Velcro closure. You can put enough tension on the strap to secure the boot on your dog's leg without over-tightening.

Bella Boots link together between use- great for organized storage and drying.

To care for your Bella Boots, simply rinse in warm water, link together and hang to dry.
