Protecting Paws since 2011

created in New York City

Lauren is a Costume Designer for TV and Film in New York, NY. She used the same process of fitting costumes for stunt actors to find a great solution for a dog boot.

After being frustrated with boots that were difficult to get on a dog and easily fell off, she set out to develop a better boot.

In cities, it is required by law that building owners de-ice their sidewalks. The salt and chemicals used for this can injure a dog's paws, and if they lick their paws, the salt and chemicals are toxic to ingest.

Bella Boots' mission is to help dogs by preventing pain and injury, and to help dog owners by solving the frustrating problem of getting boots on their dog, and then having a boot that doesn't stay on.

Bella Boots delivers: a boot that is easy to put on, stays on, protects, and feels natural.
