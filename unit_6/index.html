Bella Boots are great for older dogs. They provide secure footing and protect paws from injury. Pet parents say that their older dogs are more playful and confidant wearing Bella Boots.

Bella Boots will protect your dogs paws. Salt and chemicals on city streets and sidewalks can injure your dog's paws and are toxic if they lick their feet. Bella Boots are easy to put on and they stay on.

Bella Boots are comfortable on your dog's feet. Your dog will enjoy playing outdoors in the cold winter months.

Dogs with lots of curly fur get ice balls that hurt. Before Bella Boots Otie had to go home when there was snow, now he can stay out and play.
