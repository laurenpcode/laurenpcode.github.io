Walk, Run, Play

Bella Boots feel great on your dog's paws. Your dog can feel the ground and be themselves. They can enjoy being outdoors in all kinds of winter conditions.

Easy to put on

Bella Boots are easy to put on. They slip on easily and the Velstretch strap wraps securely. Bella Boots are organized- color coded by size and link together between wearing.

Stays on

Never lose a boot. Because of the design the boots won't slip off. Have fun in the snow and never worry about losing a boot.

Protects

Bella Boots protect your dog's paws from salt and harsh chemicals used on city streets and sidewalks. Bella Boots are also great for dogs with curly fur who get ice-balls.
